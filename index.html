I am a researcher in Animal Sciences Department at Burdur Mehmet Akif Ersoy University. My research is focused on animal welfare, on-farm biosecurity, and developing AI-assisted tools for the livestock sector. My experience includes contributing to international projects like COST Actions and Horizon Europe as well as working as a consultant for the FAO. I possess a diverse skill set that merges specialized veterinary protocols with data analysis, AI integration, education and web development. My PhD is about seasonal welfare differences of sheep along with using air quality and biosecurity measurements as welfare indicators.
